What is Kamada Ekadashi?


Kamada Ekadashi falls on the 11th day (Ekadashi) of the bright fortnight (Shukla Paksha) in the Hindu month of Chaitra. As per the latest news, this year it will be celebrated on April 2, 2026. The festival is considered one of the most important Ekadashis and offers an opportunity to seek Lord Vishnu's blessings for prosperity and success.

Significance of Kamada Ekadashi


The significance of Kamada Ekadashi lies in its association with Lord Vishnu, who is considered the preserver of the universe according to Hindu mythology. Devotees believe that performing this vrat helps fulfill their desires and attain spiritual growth. The story behind Kamada Ekadashi narrates a tale about Lord Krishna, who explained the power of this Ekadashi to his mother Yashoda.


Rituals for Parana (Breaking Fast)
